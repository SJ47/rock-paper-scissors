Welcome to Rock, Paper and Scissors

Rules of the Game

Select Play Game to start a new game

Enter your name in the player1 box then make your selection

You will be playing against the computer.

The computer will randomly select a choice after you have selected your choice

    ROCK beats SCISSORS

    PAPER beats ROCK

    SCISSORS beats PAPER

In the event that you and the computer select the same choice, it will be a draw.

Have Fun!!!
